How Can You Help?

Reduce!

  • Buy less. Before paying, decide if you really need that item.

Buying Clothes

If you need to purchase clothes, think about purchasing...

  • Second-hand clothes
  • Clothes from brands with recycling programs
  • Apparel from affordable and ethical brands

What to do with Excess Clothes

Donate to...

  • Local shelters
  • Nonprofit organizations
  • Chain thrift stores

Consider donating to local shelters and nonprofit organizations over chain thrift stores because they will give your clothes directly to the people in your community who need it. If the first two options are unavailable, donate to large chain thrift stores.
